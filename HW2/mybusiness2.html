About Us

Welcome. Luna Piao, president and founder of Luna's® Coffee. I am a full-time student of the University of California, Los Angeles, majoring in Financial Actuarial Mathematics and pursuing a Specialization in Computing. From a year ago, I have been running this company while studying mathematics and computer programming courses at UCLA to earn some extra income to cover my college expenses.

Drinking coffee has always been my favorite activity of all. Living in Los Angeles for the past five years, I have been fortunate enough to have had the opportunity to taste many different types of coffee. Some were from specialty coffee shops, some were from local coffee shops. I wanted to make my own coffee in my own coffee shop. I wanted to make a type of coffee that is different from all other coffee in the world. After months of experimentation, I finally found that a custom blend of three coffee beans from three different countries would make the coffee taste one-of-a-kind and unforgettable. I would love to share this extraordinary new coffee with every coffee lover in our community!
